NGBS-001

Business Excellence: From Branding to Sales Mastery

This course equips aspiring entrepreneurs and professionals with the tools needed to build a powerful brand, develop irresistible products, and master persuasive sales techniques to scale their businesses.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamentals of branding and brand positioning
  • Learn how to develop customer-focused products
  • Gain practical skills in persuasive and ethical sales strategies

Course Modules

01

Introduction to Business Success Principles

Explore the key mindsets, habits, and foundational frameworks that successful entrepreneurs use to build and grow sustainable ventures.

02

Crafting a Compelling Brand Identity

Discover how to define your brand's mission, voice, and visual identity. Learn the principles of consistency, storytelling, and emotional branding that drive recognition and trust.

03

Product Development & Market Fit

Understand how to research your market, identify customer pain points, and develop products that align with real needs. Learn to apply lean validation techniques and gather feedback for product improvement.

04

Sales Psychology and Consumer Behavior

Explore how buyers make decisions and what motivates them to act. Learn how to use psychological triggers ethically to build desire, overcome objections, and position your offer effectively.

05

Sales Funnel and Lead Generation

Build a customer journey from awareness to purchase. Learn strategies for generating leads through content, landing pages, email, and social media, and how to nurture them with automated funnels.

06

Closing Deals & Building Loyalty

Master effective closing techniques and post-sale engagement. Learn how to turn buyers into repeat customers through relationship-building, onboarding, and brand loyalty programs.

Learning Outcomes

  • Launch or rebrand a business with clarity and confidence
  • Develop and test products aligned with market needs
  • Communicate and sell effectively to different customer types
  • Be commercially aware, with a strong understanding of market dynamics, competitive positioning, and brand equity
  • Be productive using free business collaboration tools such as Trello, Notion, Canva, and Google Workspace to manage projects, communicate, and create marketing assets

Ideal For

Entrepreneurs, startup founders, marketing and sales professionals
