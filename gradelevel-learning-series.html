K-12 Learning Series

Nex Genix Business School's K-12 Learning Series is a structured, one-on-one tutoring program designed to help students from Kindergarten through Grade 12 excel in their core subjects. Our licensed tutors deliver engaging, personalized sessions aligned with the DepEd K-12 curriculum, ensuring every student gets the attention and guidance they deserve. Upon completing the program, students receive an authenticated certificate with a verification code that can be validated through our Certificate Verification Page.

Whether your child needs academic support during the school year or wants to make the most of summer break, our flexible packages and expert tutors are ready to help them level up.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling

What grade levels does the K-12 Series cover?
Our K-12 Series covers all grade levels from Kindergarten through Grade 12, with sessions tailored to each student's specific curriculum and learning needs.
Are the tutors licensed?
Yes — all NGBS tutors are licensed professionals with verified teaching credentials and experience in K-12 subject areas.
How are sessions conducted?
All sessions are conducted online via video call, making it convenient for students anywhere in the Philippines or abroad. You only need a stable internet connection and a device.
Can I enroll in more than one subject?
Absolutely! Our Standard and Intensive packages allow enrollment in 2 to 3 subjects. You can also coordinate a custom multi-subject plan with our enrollment team.
Will my child receive a certificate?
Yes! Upon completing the program, students receive an authenticated digital certificate with a unique verification code that can be verified through our Certificate Verification Page.
